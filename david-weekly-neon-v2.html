Sync with New DRI — Xiaolin

I've been spending time doing 1:1s with Xiaolin, to better understand the current situation for Copilot Mac, his priorities, and where design can best support the effort.

His near-term goal is to bring all features that are already GA in CMC to GA on Mac within the next two months. The immediate milestone is to push the current feature list to GA within the next two weeks. This milestone will also play an important role in informing the broader discussion around the long-term direction of the Mac client.

Given the timeline, we aligned that in the short term we may need to slightly relax the quality bar to ensure speed of delivery — focusing on making sure the core screens and flows are solid, and leaving room for refinement and polish afterward.
